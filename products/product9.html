Product Name: MG-Q10 (Tablets)

Product Information

MG Q10 aids in increasing the oxygen supply to the brain. It is a blend of riboflavin, magnesium and coenzyme Q10 and vitamin D3 that can aid with migraines, headaches, intolerance to loud noise, eye, neck and head pain and lightheadedness. This medication is classified as a nutritional supplement and is used to address nutritional deficiencies when the body seems unable to absorb or obtain adequate nutrients from foods. Nutritional shortage ensures improper bone growth, anaemia, skin abnormalities, digestive troubles, neurological disorders and dementia are all possible side effects. It’s a combination of nutrients and an antioxidant that helps cells generate energy. Magnesium is a mineral that helps muscles and neurons work normally, Riboflavin helps in cellular respiration and aids in normal cell growth and function.

Safety Precautions

1. Store in a cool and dry place.

2. Keep away from the eyes.

3. Keep out of the reach of children.

4. Do not use if the seal is missing or damaged.

Effects of Deficiency

1. Muscle cramps and spasms.

2. Fatigue and weakness.

3. Numbness or tingling sensations.

4. Sleep disturbances and restlessness.

5. Increased risk of migraines.

6. Low energy and fatigue – due to reduced ATP production in cells.

7. Heart problems – since the heart muscle requires a lot of energy, low CoQ10 can contribute to heart failure, hypertension, or palpitations.

8. Cracked lips and corners of the mouth (cheilosis).

9. Sore throat and redness of the tongue (glossitis).

10. Skin disorders – dryness, itching, or scaling.

11. Slow growth and poor healing of tissues.

12. Neurological symptoms in severe deficiency (confusion, irritability).

Key Ingredients
